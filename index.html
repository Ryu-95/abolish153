What is the purpose of this website?

this website is here to represent a safe space and support group for victims or potential victims of domestic abuse. Navigate through our services to talk to other victims and survivors, book a shelter to stay safe in, or register your own space for someone in need. Contact a counsler or a lawyer and read through frequently asked questions.

What Are the Signs of Domestic Abuse?

-Your partner bullies, threatens, or controls you

-Your partner controls your money

-Your partner cuts you off from family and friends

-Your partner physically abuses you
